
# First Check — LEAKING DOORS

## Symptom
- Water leaking from doors when closed  

---

## Decision Steps

### Step 1 — Leakage Present?
Is leakage present with doors closed?

- **YES →** Step 2  
- **NO →**
  - No fault present  

---

### Step 2 — Allowable Rate
Is leakage greater than **1 drop every 2–3 seconds per door**?

- **YES →** Step 3  
- **NO →**
  - **Monitor condition**

---

### Step 3 — Leakage Pattern
Is leakage uneven or localised?

- **YES →**
  - **Workshop inspection required**

- **NO →**
  - **Workshop inspection required**

---

## Outcomes
- Monitor condition  
- Workshop inspection required
