# First Check — SNORKEL PUMP WON’T OPERATE

**Source:** troubleshooting_guide.pdf  
**Document:** McDav-FFTTG  
**Revision:** 03  
**Pages:** 13  

---

## Symptom
- Snorkel Pump won’t operate  
- Tank otherwise functions normally  

---

## Preconditions / Notes
- System power available  
- Isolair Display Box operational  

---

## Decision Steps

### Step 1 — Control Box Check
**Question:**  
Is the green **DOOR CLOSE** LED illuminated on the Isolair Display Box?

- **YES →** Proceed to Step 2  
- **NO →** Proceed to Step 3  

---

### Step 2 — Cyclic Controller Check
**Question:**  
Trigger the Pump Switch in both directions on the Cyclic Controller.  
Does the Snorkel Pump operate in either direction (one direction only)?

- **YES →**
  - Pump Switch is faulty but partially functional  
  - Continue use if necessary, but replace ASAP  

- **NO →** Proceed to Step 4  

---

### Step 3 — Pressure Switch Check
**Question:**  
Change the Pressure Switch. Does the Snorkel Pump now operate?

- **YES →**
  - Faulty Pressure Switch identified  

- **NO →**
  - Begin detailed troubleshooting at **Pressure Switch Check** (through checks)

---

### Step 4 — Snorkel Pump Power Relay Check
**Question:**  
When the Pump Switch is triggered, can you hear the Snorkel Pump Power Relay energise?  
*(A distinct clicking noise should be heard inside the tank control box.)*

> **NOTE**  
> This step confirms whether the control signal is reaching the relay.

- **YES →** Proceed to Step 6  
- **NO →** Proceed to Step 5  

---

### Step 5 — Cyclic Controller Substitution
**Question:**  
Change the Cyclic Controller. Does the Snorkel Pump now operate?

- **YES →**
  - Faulty Cyclic Controller (Pump Switch circuit)  
  - Secure serviceable controller to remain operational  
  - Tag faulty unit for workshop repair ASAP  

- **NO →**
  - Likely fault with Snorkel Pump Power Relay, associated wiring, or control circuit  
  - Begin detailed troubleshooting at **Snorkel Pump Power Relay Check**

---

### Step 6 — Snorkel Pump Substitution
**Question:**  
Change the Snorkel Pump with a serviceable spare and re-test.  
Does the replacement Snorkel Pump operate?

- **YES →**
  - Faulty Snorkel Pump identified  
  - Continue to use replacement pump  
  - Faulty pump to be investigated by Tank Technician or Avionics at base  
  - Begin detailed troubleshooting at **Snorkel Pump Motor Check**

- **NO →**
  - Wiring fault likely  
  - Begin detailed troubleshooting at **Snorkel Pump Won’t Operate** (through checks)

---

## Outcomes & Diagnoses
- **Faulty Pressure Switch** — Perform Pressure Switch Check  
- **Faulty Cyclic Controller (Pump Switch)** — Replace Cyclic Controller  
- **Faulty Snorkel Pump Motor or ECC** — Perform Snorkel Pump Motor Check  
- **Faulty Snorkel Pump Power Relay** — Perform Snorkel Pump Power Relay Check  
- **Wiring fault** — Perform Snorkel Pump Won’t Operate (through checks)
