---

## Symptom
- Water leaking from one or more tank doors when doors are in the **closed** position

---

## Preconditions / Notes
> **IMPORTANT**  
> Tank door adjustment and rigging are **workshop-level tasks only**.  
> Door cable or seal adjustment must only be carried out by trained personnel.

---

## Decision Steps

### Step 1 — Leakage Confirmation
**Question:**  
Is water leaking from the tank doors when the doors are commanded **closed**?

- **YES →** Proceed to Step 2  
- **NO →**
  - Condition not present  
  - No further action required  

---

### Step 2 — Allowable Leakage Check
**Question:**  
Is the leakage rate **greater than 1 drop every 2–3 seconds per door**?

> **NOTE**  
> A leakage rate of **up to 1 drop every 2–3 seconds per door** is considered acceptable and does not require adjustment.

- **YES →** Proceed to Step 3  
- **NO →**
  - Leakage within allowable limits  
  - Continue service and monitor condition  

---

### Step 3 — Leakage Pattern Check
**Question:**  
Is the leakage uneven, localised to a single door, or significantly greater on one side?

- **YES →**
  - Door seal wear or cable tension issue suspected  
  - Workshop inspection required  

- **NO →**
  - General seal wear likely  
  - Workshop inspection required  

---

## Outcomes & Disposition
- **Leakage within allowable limits** — Continue service and monitor  
- **Leakage exceeds allowable limits** — Workshop inspection required  
- **Uneven or abnormal leakage** — Workshop inspection required
