5. LEAKING DOORS
5.1.1 - Doors may leak after a lot of use, or depending on the quality of the water source.
First put the aircraft on wheels to tilt back for easier access to tank doors. ENSURE THAT
AIRCRAFT POWER IS OFF! Pry doors open and check door seals (be careful as doors are on
return springs and there will be some resistance). Door seals may look torn along where the
door runs, this can be normal and may still be fine as long as no section of seal is missing
and nothing is stuck in the seals. If they do have some obvious problem, replace that section
of the door seal with the equipment in the fire tank spares kit, or the entire seal if needed.
The tank will need to be removed from the aircraft to replace a seal.
If the seals look good, it may be that a cable/s need adjusting to put more tension on that
section of the door.

DO NOT ATTEMPT TO ADJUST THE DOORS WITHOUT HAVING THE PROPER TRAINING AS
YOU MAY WORSEN THE PROBLEM. (see pic 46)
If the doors need to be adjusted, please ensure it is completed by someone who knows how
to adjust the doors properly, or have trained personnel talk you through the process.
