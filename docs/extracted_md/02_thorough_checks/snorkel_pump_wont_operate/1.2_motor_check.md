# 1.2 — Snorkel Pump Motor Check
**System:** Snorkel Pump Won’t Operate  
**Source:** troubleshooting_guide.pdf (pp. 18)

---

## Purpose
Verify that the snorkel pump motor and power cable are functioning correctly.

---

## Preconditions / Notes
- System ON
- **DOOR CLOSE** LED illuminated (pressure in range) before testing

---

## Step 1 — Power at Pump Connector
**Question:** When the Pump Switch is triggered, is there power and ground at the snorkel pump power receptacle (tank side)?

### Test Method
- Disconnect the **Snorkel Pump Anderson Plug (214)** from the tank receptacle.
- While another person operates the **Pump Switch**, check:
  - For power at the tank-mounted receptacle
  - That a good ground is present at the other terminal
- Refer to **Pic 2**, **Pic 2.1**, **Pic 2.2**, and **Pic 3** for connector locations and examples.

### Result
- **YES →**
  - Pump assembly is suspect (pump/ECC or cable/connector).
  - Swap to the spare pump assembly to remain serviceable.
  - Tag the removed pump/cable for workshop troubleshooting.
  - Proceed to **Step 2 — Cable Continuity Check (1.2.2)** when practical.

- **NO →**
  - Pump motor is likely not the issue.
  - Proceed to **1.3 — Pressure Switch Check**.

---

## Step 2 — Cable Continuity Check (1.2.2)
**Question:** Is there continuity through both the power and ground wires in the pump power cable?

### Test Method
- Disconnect:
  - The **Pump Motor Milspec Plug**
  - The **Anderson Plug (214)** from the tank
- Check both power and ground wires for continuity.
- Ensure Anderson plug contacts are properly locked into the housing (see **Pic 2**).

### Result
- **YES →**
  - Power cable is serviceable.
  - Motor is faulty, most likely the **End Cap Controller (ECC)**.
  - Continue using the spare pump.
  - Replace the ECC as soon as possible.
  - **NOTE:**
    - All spare ECCs are programmed for **214 snorkels only**.
    - ECC programming must be completed by an authorised technician.

- **NO →**
  - Faulty power cable or Anderson plug.
  - Investigate wiring and connectors further.

---

## Connector Reference
- **214 Tank:** Snorkel pump power receptacle — Anderson plug (left-hand side of tank)
- **Loom identification**
  - Top of loom: Anderson plug
  - Bottom of loom: Milspec plug
