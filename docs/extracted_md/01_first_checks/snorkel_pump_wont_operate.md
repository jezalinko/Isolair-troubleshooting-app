# First Check — SNORKEL PUMP WON’T OPERATE

**Source:** troubleshooting_guide.pdf  
**Document:** McDav-FFTTG  
**Revision:** 03  
**Pages:** 13

---

## Symptom
- Snorkel Pump won’t operate  
- Tank otherwise functions normally

---

## Preconditions / Notes
- System power available  
- Isolair Display Box operational  

---

## Decision Steps

### Step 1
**Action:** Control Box Check  
**Question:** Is the green “DOOR CLOSE” LED illuminated on the Isolair Display Box?

- **YES →** Proceed to Step 2  
- **NO →** Proceed to Step 3  

---

### Step 2
**Action:** Cyclic Controller Check  
**Question:** Trigger the Pump Switch in both directions on the Cyclic Controller.  
Does the Snorkel Pump operate in either direction (one direction only)?

- **YES →**
  - Pump Switch is faulty but still operates in one direction  
  - Continue use if necessary, but replace ASAP  

- **NO →** Proceed to Step 4  

---

### Step 3
**Action:** Pressure Switch Check  
**Question:** Change the Pressure Switch. Does the Snorkel Pump now work?

- **YES →**
  - Pressure Switch was faulty  

- **NO →**
  - Begin a thorough check at **Para. 1.3.1 — Pressure Switch Check**

---

### Step 4
**Action:** Power Relay Check  
**Question:** Can you hear the Snorkel Pump Power Relay energize when you trigger the Pump Switch?  
*(A distinct clicking noise will be heard inside the tank control box)*

- **YES →** Proceed to Step 6  
- **NO →** Proceed to Step 5  

---

### Step 5
**Action:** Cyclic Controller Check  
**Question:** Change the Cyclic Controller. Does the Snorkel Pump now operate?

- **YES →**
  - Faulty Cyclic Controller (Pump Switch)  
  - Secure new Cyclic Controller to remain serviceable  
  - Tag faulty unit for workshop replacement ASAP  

- **NO →**
  - Most likely a fault with the Snorkel Pump Power Relay, Snorkel Pump Slave Relay (if fitted), or wiring circuit  
  - Begin a thorough check at **Para. 1.4.3 — Snorkel Pump Power Relay Check**

---

### Step 6
**Action:** Snorkel Pump Check  
**Question:** Change the Snorkel Pump and try again. Does the new Snorkel Pump work?

- **YES →**
  - Faulty Snorkel Pump  
  - Continue to use the new pump  
  - Troubleshoot faulty pump ASAP  
  - Begin at **Para. 1.2.2 — Snorkel Pump Motor Check**

- **NO →**
  - Most likely a wiring fault  
  - Begin a thorough check at the start of **Part 1 — Snorkel Pump Won’t Operate**

---

## Outcomes & Diagnoses
- **Faulty Pressure Switch** — Para. 1.3.1  
- **Faulty Cyclic Controller (Pump Switch)** — Para. 1.6.1  
- **Faulty Snorkel Pump Motor** — Para. 1.2.1  
- **Faulty Snorkel Pump Power Relay** — Para. 1.4.1  
- **Wiring fault** — Begin at start of Part 1
