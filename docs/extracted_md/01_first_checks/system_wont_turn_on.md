# First Check — SYSTEM WON’T TURN ON  
*(Hydraulic Pump Won’t Run)*

**Source:** troubleshooting_guide.pdf  
**Document:** McDav-FFTTG  
**Revision:** 03  
**Pages:** 14

---

## Symptom
- System will not turn on  
- Hydraulic pump does not run

---

## Preconditions / Notes
- Control Box ON/OFF switch set to ON  
- Milspec plugs correctly connected  

---

## Decision Steps

### Step 1 — Display Check
**Question:** Are any LEDs illuminated on the Isolair Display Box?

- **YES →** Proceed to Step 2  
- **NO →** Proceed to Step 3  

---

### Step 2 — Jettison Switch Check
**Action:** Toggle the Jettison Switch a few times and ensure it is in the normal operating position (UP).  
**Question:** Does the system now turn on?

- **YES →**
  - Jettison Switch was in the OFF position or was beginning to fail  
  - Replace Cyclic Controller  

- **NO →** Proceed to Step 4  

---

### Step 3 — Circuit Breaker Check
**Question:** Have any of the Circuit Breakers on the Isolair Display Box popped?

- **YES →**
  - Reset breakers and re-try  
  - If system still does not turn on, proceed to Step 5  

- **NO →** Proceed to Step 7  

---

### Step 4 — Cyclic Controller Check
**Action:** Change Cyclic Controller  
**Question:** Does the system now turn on?

- **YES →**
  - Faulty Cyclic Controller  
  - Tag faulty unit for workshop repair  
  - Workshop technician to use Wiring Diagram 6.1 to identify which internal switch failed  

- **NO →** Proceed to Step 6  

---

### Step 5 — Aircraft Power Check
**Question:** Are you operating off ground power? (Engines not running)

- **YES →** Proceed to Step 7  
- **NO →**
  - Check all relevant aircraft Circuit Breakers  
  - Continue with **Para. 2.2 — Circuit Breaker Checks**

---

### Step 6 — Pressure Switch Check
**Action:** Change Pressure Switch  
**Question:** Does the system now turn on?

- **YES →**
  - Pressure Switch was faulty  
  - Discard

- **NO →**
  - Most likely a wiring or relay fault  
  - Begin **Para. 2.4.1 — Tank Power Supply Check**

---

### Step 7 — Bus Power Check
**Action:** Ensure the **NON-ESSENTIAL BUS** is set to **MANUAL**  
**Question:** Does the system now turn on?

- **YES →**
  - On Bell 214 aircraft, **MANUAL must always be selected when using ground power**

- **NO →**
  - Check all relevant aircraft Circuit Breakers  
  - Continue with **Para. 2.2 — Circuit Breaker Checks**

---

## Outcomes & Diagnoses
- **Faulty Circuit Breakers** — Para. 2.2.1  
- **Faulty Cyclic Controller** — Replace Cyclic Controller  
- **Faulty Pressure Switch** — Replace or perform Tank Power Supply Check (Para. 2.4.1)  
- **Faulty Hydraulic Pump Relays** — Para. 2.7.1  
- **Faulty Wiring** — Para. 2.8.5
